# Simple AI-powered Recruitment tool for SMB

## Tech Stack:
- GSheets, GDrive, GDoc, GForms
- Chatbot (Claude?) MCPs if required
- Apps script if required OR/AND **internal MCP**.

## Workflow (draft)

- Chatbot: [user request of recruitment].

- Chatbot or Form: [questions about the need].

- AI backstage: [use [unclear/crossed-out] answers + job desc. template to output a job desc. -> store in Google Drive -> provide URL to user]

- [crossed-out] Chatbot: [request user sign-off status (validated, draft)]

- AI Backstage: [create row in a Google Sheet with JD URL, key info, status + email or "slack-like" message to HR].

- Google Sheets: [HR team validates + select where to post/send the JD].

- Workflow & AI backstage: [JD is posted + sent by email if selected] [A form for candidate is available].

- Candidates answer form -> Google Sheets fed + CV stored in G. Drive.

- AI pre-analyzes the CV -> pre-recommend (Reject/Approve/Arbitrate) ->

2

- HR team validate candidates to meet.

- A.I prepares draft mail, customized per candidate -> HR validate -> workflow sends the mail (from an HR mailbox).

- A.I scans HR mailbox answers -> prepare draft mail + meetings invitation -> warns HR team.

- Once appointment approved or rejected, workflow update gSheets + validates calendar meeting.

- For approved appointments, AI prepares the pros/cons of application (CV vs Job Desc), and questions to ask.

- AI reviews comments/interview, fact-checks against JD, proposes ranking -
